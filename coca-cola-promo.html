From 1886 to Today

1886 – The Beginning

Invented by Dr. John S. Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia, Coca-Cola was first sold at Jacob’s Pharmacy.

1928 – Olympic Debut

Coca-Cola becomes an official sponsor of the Olympics, showing its global aspirations.

1985 – Coke in Space

Coca-Cola becomes the first soft drink consumed in space aboard the Space Shuttle Challenger.

2025 – Still the Icon

More than 1.9 billion servings of Coca-Cola products are enjoyed every day worldwide.
